### Final Thoughts {#0c9f .graf .graf--h3 .graf--leading name="0c9f"}

So, what if hackers really had HR departments?

Well, maybe breaches would come with **performance reports**, rogue
ransomware devs would get *warnings for insubordination*, and phishing
emails would be grammatically accurate (finally!).

But more importantly...

Maybe they'd *finally* stop ghosting people after a job is done.

And while the idea of a hacker HR department is completely ridiculous,
absurd, and makes absolutely no sense...

So does the fact that **your uncle still uses "password123"** for his
bank login.

So maybe, just maybe, we're not too far from this cyber sitcom becoming
real life.
