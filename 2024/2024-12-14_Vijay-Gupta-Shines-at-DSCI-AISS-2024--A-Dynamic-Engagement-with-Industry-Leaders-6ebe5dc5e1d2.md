### Recognizing Excellence {#285e .graf .graf--h3 .graf-after--p name="285e"}

The summit was also a platform for celebrating achievements in the
cybersecurity domain. Among the highlights was the Cyberabad Police
Commissionerate's win of the *DSCI Excellence Award*, acknowledging
their pioneering efforts in strengthening cybersecurity measures and
promoting cyber safety. This recognition underscores the importance of
initiatives that align with DSCI's mission to foster a secure digital
ecosystem.

### About DSCI AISS 2024 {#eec4 .graf .graf--h3 .graf-after--p name="eec4"}

The Annual Information Security Summit (AISS) is a flagship event by the
Data Security Council of India, designed to bring together policymakers,
practitioners, and innovators to discuss pivotal developments in the
field of cybersecurity. For more details on the event, visit [AISS 2024
Official Website](https://www.dsci.in/event/aiss-2024/){.markup--anchor
.markup--p-anchor data-href="https://www.dsci.in/event/aiss-2024/"
