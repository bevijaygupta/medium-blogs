### What is a VPN (Virtual Private Network)? {#d583 .graf .graf--h3 .graf-after--li name="d583"}

A **VPN is a secure tunnel** between your device and the internet. It
encrypts your traffic and routes it through a VPN server, hiding your IP
address from the websites you visit and your ISP.

Unlike TOR, which distributes traffic through random volunteers' nodes,
**VPNs rely on centralized servers** managed by a company.

You connect to one of their servers (you choose the location), and from
there, your traffic goes out into the open internet.
