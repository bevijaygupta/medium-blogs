The landscape of cybersecurity continues to evolve rapidly, and with it,
the tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs) employed by Red Teams to
emulate real-world cyber threats. Whether you're a seasoned Red Teamer
or just beginning your journey in offensive security, the right tools
are critical to executing successful simulations. This blog outlines an
arsenal of Red Team tools categorized by their purposes, including
reconnaissance, initial access, delivery, and more. Let's dive into
these tools and their use cases.
